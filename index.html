About

Martin Reed is a Senior Lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering at the University of Essex, Colchester, UK. His main research interests are the fields of Computer Networks, Network Control, Multimedia transmission over networks and Network Security. His teaching duties are also in these areas.
